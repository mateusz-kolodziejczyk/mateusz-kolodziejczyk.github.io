What this site is

We are a charity shop based in New Ross, giving back to the community and thriving off of community support and donations. We aren't associated with any charity group, and are fully independent as a result.

Who we are

We've been here for more than ten years, giving back to the community from direct donations and money from sales. We've grown a lot since we started, and are now known as one of the biggest charity shops in Wexford.

What we do

We raise money from donations and sales of products donated to give back to people in the local community. 90% of money raised is donated to local causes, including new buildings and charity initiatives for poor and homeless people. The rest is all used to buy products and keep the shop afloat.

What you can do to help

You can choose to donate money or items to us to help us raise money for New Ross. More information can be found on the donation page on this website.
