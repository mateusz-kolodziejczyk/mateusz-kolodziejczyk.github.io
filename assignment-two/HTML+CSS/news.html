New sponsorship with local soccer club

We are proud to announce a new sponsorship with a local soccer cup, which will involve a small patch on their jersey for the next season. We wish to get the word out about our organisation as we depend on your support to thrive as we are a charity organisation.

New aisle

Last week we were able to build a new aisle in our shop, all thanks to your support. This will help the overflow of donated items over the last few weeks, where they had to be stored in a local warehouse due to your unbounded generosity.

Online Donations now Accepted

The online donation facility has been launched today, no longer will you have to come in person in order to donate to us. We would like for everyone reading this to consider giving even one Euro to us, as we depend on donations such as these to survive.

New Website Launch

This is the first news article written on this redesigned website, our last website not having been updated since 2006. We hope you enjoy the more modern design and easier to navigate pages.
