Why you should donate

As we are a non-profit organisation we depend on you for our continuing existence. If you would be so inclined you can choose to donate here.

Monetary Donations

If you wish to donate any money, you can donate here online through this form, or you can drop off the money at our shop. If you wish to donate anonymously put in anonymous as your first name or drop it off at the donation box in front of the shop

Non-Monetary Donations

Donate all non-monetary donations in person at our shop at the address provided down below. If you wish for your donation to remain anonymous drop it in the bag in front of the shop. Here is a table to give you an example of what kind of items we accept and don't accept:

Accepted Not Accepted
Clothes of all kinds Food
Blankets and Duvets Electrical Toys
Regular toys and plushies Electric appliances
Books Personal Care Products(Deodorants etc)
